============================================================
                ===[GUILDS OF THE WORLD]===

(1) Warrior: Champion of sword and shield, the warrior relies 
             on physical prowess to withstand enemies and 
             deliver crushing strikes, excelling in melee.
(2) Cleric:  Gifted with powers by the gods, the cleric 
             excels in channeling holy might to struggle 
             against evil.
(3) Rogue:   Masters of stealth, the rogue thrives in 
             mystery, dancing through battle with deadly
             precision.
(4) Wizard:  Harnessing elemental power, the wizard uses 
             magical arts to strike from a distance, though
             they themselves are often weak in hand-to-hand.
(5) Ranger:  Stalking the wilds on the edge of civilization,
             rangers are the ultimate survivalists, often 
             braving treacherous terrain and deadly beasts.
