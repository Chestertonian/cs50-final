Syntax: 

'attack <target>'

------------------------------------

Combat is turn-based. Each round, you and your opponent exchange actions until one side is defeated.

On your turn, you may:
- Cast a spell
- Use an ability or item (if available)
- Take any other action.

In between turns, you and your enemy will strike at one another with your weapons (or bare hands.)

Hit chance is determined by your combat skills and attributes. Even strong attacks may sometimes miss, and weaker enemies may occasionally land hits.

Damage is reduced by armor and defensive effects. All damage has a small variance to make combat less predictable.

Fights are designed to last several rounds. Strong enemies may require multiple tactics.

Multiple enemies may attack at once in group encounters.
