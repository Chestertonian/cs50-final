Syntax: 

'score' shows experience gained.

------------------------------------

Experience points (XP) are awarded for defeating enemies.

Stronger enemies grant more XP. Weaker enemies grant less.

XP is based on the relative difficulty of the enemy compared to your level.

Some NPCs do not grant XP if they are not intended as combat targets.

Group encounters may yield higher XP rewards depending on how many enemies are defeated.

XP is what determines progression toward the next level.
