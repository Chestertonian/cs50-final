Your resources naturally regenerate over time.

Health may regenerate through healing abilities, depending on your situation.

SP will regenerate if you wait.

Movement Points (MP) also regenerate over time and are used for...movement.

Regeneration ensures that you can continue adventuring without requiring constant resting, but careful resource management is still extremely important.

Related files: help mana, help leveling, help combat.
