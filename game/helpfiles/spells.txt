Syntax: 

'powername' (e.g. 'magicmissile.') No spaces.

------------------------------------

Spells are special abilities that consume SP (check your SP by typing 'hp.')

Some spells target a single enemy, while others affect multiple targets.

Spells may deal damage, heal, protect, or alter combat conditions.

Spell effectiveness may depend on your attributes such as INT or WIS.

Higher-level spells cost more SP but provide much greater impact.

Related files: help mana, help combat, help regeneration, help SP.
