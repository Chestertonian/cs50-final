Syntax: 

'cataclysm <target>'

------------------------------------

Cataclysm is a devastating spell that unleashes the most terrifying elemental energies of creation on a single target. It affects one enemy and deals extremely high damage.

As it consumes a massive amount of SP, it is not intended for frequent use. This spell represents one of the most powerful known applications of raw arcane energy.

Use of this spell can decisively end battles, but improper use may leave the caster vulnerable afterward, even turning on the caster to injure them.
