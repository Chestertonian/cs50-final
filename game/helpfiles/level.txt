You gain experience points (XP) by defeating enemies

When you gain enough XP, you will level up automatically.

Each level increases your:
- Maximum Health (HP)
- Maximum Spell Power (SP)
- Maximum Movement Points (MP)

You also gain improved combat effectiveness through your skills.

Higher levels require significantly more XP. Progression is fast early on and becomes slower over time.

Leveling is the primary method of character growth in this game.
