Syntax: 

'hp'

------------------------------------

SP is the resource used to cast spells/use skills.

Each spell has a SP cost. You must have enough SP to cast the spell.

You begin with a small amount of SP, and it increases as you level up.

SP is shared across all spellcasting/skill abilities, meaning you must manage it carefully during combat.

If you run out of SP, you cannot cast spells until it is restored.

SP represents your magical or mental energy depending on your character type.

Related files: help spells, help regeneration, help combat.
